Who I Am

Designing with
empathy & intention

I'm a product-oriented UX/UI Designer based in Chennai, India, with a deep belief that great design is invisible — it simply works. My background spans fintech, healthcare, energy, and service platforms, where I've learned that the best experiences emerge at the intersection of user needs and business goals.

I approach every project as a problem to be solved, not a canvas to be decorated. That means diving deep into user research, questioning assumptions, and designing with data — then crafting interfaces that feel effortless.

"Design is not what it looks like. Design is how it works — and how it makes people feel."
— My Design Philosophy
  • Research-first mindset: every pixel has a reason
  • Cross-functional collaboration in agile teams
  • Systems thinking for scalable design patterns
  • Accessibility and inclusivity from day one
